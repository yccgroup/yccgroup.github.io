About us

Computational molecular science across scales

Our group studies molecular behavior with a combination of statistical mechanics, molecular simulation, theoretical chemistry, and machine learning. We are especially interested in methods that connect microscopic molecular interactions to experimentally meaningful observables.

We welcome students and collaborators who enjoy mathematical thinking, programming, molecular simulation, and interdisciplinary scientific questions.

We are recruiting

Prospective graduate students, undergraduate researchers, and postdoctoral collaborators are encouraged to get in touch. Prior experience with Python, molecular simulation, statistical mechanics, quantum chemistry, or machine learning is helpful, but curiosity and persistence matter most.
