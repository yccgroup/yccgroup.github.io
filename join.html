Join us

We are looking for motivated students and collaborators

The group welcomes people who enjoy theory, computation, molecular science, and careful scientific communication.

Graduate students

Students interested in theoretical chemistry, molecular simulation, statistical mechanics, quantum dynamics, biophysics, or machine learning for molecular science are encouraged to contact the group.

Undergraduate researchers

Undergraduate projects can involve programming, literature review, molecular modeling, data analysis, or development of small research tools.

Postdoctoral fellows and visitors

Researchers with experience in molecular simulation, theoretical chemistry, computational biology, or scientific software are welcome to discuss possible projects and fellowships.

How to inquire

Please send a brief email with your CV, research interests, and a short description of your background. For student inquiries, include your current program or degree status and any relevant coursework or project experience.

Helpful background

  • Programming in Python, Julia, C/C++, or similar languages
  • Physical chemistry, statistical mechanics, or quantum mechanics
  • Molecular dynamics, computational biology, or machine learning
  • Curiosity, patience, and willingness to learn
